Policies

We aim to be flexible and fair. These guidelines help us set expectations and ensure a smooth experience for everyone.

Cancellation

24-4 hours notice: 50% of the first hour fee will be charged. Under 4 hours or no access: the full first hour fee applies. Weather or illness exceptions may apply at our discretion.

Access

Smart lock or lockbox access is preferred. Please provide clear instructions at the time of booking. All keys are logged and treated with care.

Pet Safety

A leash or harness is required for walks. For in-home care, we request up-to-date vaccination information. We follow a safety protocol for pets with aggressive tendencies.

After-Hours & Rush Requests

Requests outside of standard hours (8am-7pm) incur an additional fee of $5/hr. Rush or same-day services may require a $10 flat fee and pre-authorization of payment.

Supplies & Surfaces

We bring eco-friendly cleaning supplies and equipment. For specialty surfaces or products you prefer, please let us know in advance.

If you have any questions about these policies, please contact us. We are happy to talk through any concerns.
