Frequently Asked Questions

What are your minimums and travel fees?
We have a 2‑hour minimum per visit. Zone A (0–10 miles) no fee; Zone B (10–20 miles) +$10; Zone C (20–30 miles) +$20.
How do cancellations work?
24–4 hours: 50% first hour fee. Under 4 hours or no‑access: 100% first hour. Weather/illness exceptions apply.
Do I need to be home?
Not required. Please provide smart lock or lockbox access instructions in your booking.
Are you insured and background‑checked?
Yes — fully insured with background‑checked pros. We carry a satisfaction guarantee on every visit.
Do you handle pets?
Absolutely. We love pets. For safety, leash/harness is required for walks and we ask for vaccination attestation for in‑home care.
